Description

Perfect for the holidays!

Ingredients

  • evaporated milk
  • butterscotch sauce
  • whipped butter
  • vanilla cream soda

Steps

  2. Heat in microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Remove and stir until butter has melted and incorporated into mixture.
  5. Divide butterscotch mixture between 2 (10 to 12-ounce) mugs.
